  • international apostolic youth summit

    11-15 Sept 2019 Mlandizi-Kibaha

    The International Apostolic Youth Summit is a youth conference where young people from all over Tanzania, as well as, outside of Tanzania come together to learn the ways of the Kingdom of God and how to integrate them into their daily lives in order to impact society spiritually, socially and economically. Apart from learning the word of God, we have many other fun and interesting sessions like Praise and Worship, Drama, a talent search competition, and time to relax and socialize and make new friends.
    We warmly welcome you to this year's Generation Impact Conference. We would love to see you!
