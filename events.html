  • international apostolic youth summit

    11-15 Sept 2019 Mlandizi-Kibaha

    International apostolic youth summit is a congregation of youth from all over Tanzania as well as out of Tanzania getting together to learn ways of the Kingdom of God and how to operate them into our day to day lives so that to impact our societies spiritually, socially as well as economically. Apart from Learning the word of God we will also have other fun and interesting sessions e.g; Praise and Worship, Drama, Talent search competetion, time to socialize and expand your network of friends and many more..
    We warmly welcome you to this year's Generation Impact, We will be Happy to see you!
