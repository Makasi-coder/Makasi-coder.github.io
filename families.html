about us

What is Generation Impact?

Generation impact is an annual congregation of youth from all over Tanzania as well as out of Tanzania getting together to learn ways of the Kingdom of God and how to operate them into our day to day lives so that to impact our societies spiritually, socially as well as economically. It was founded and first held on September 2017 at Mlandizi-Kibaha Coast region. Since then it has been held on September every year. Through Generation Impact youths get enough time to study the word of God (the bible), singing, dancing and having fun together as well as getting opportunity to know one another and expand network of friends as sons and daughters in the kingdom. This year's Generation Impact will be held on 11th to 15th of september 2019. YOU ARE WARMLY WELCOME!
