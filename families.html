about us

What is Generation Impact?

Generation impact is an annual congregation of youth from all over Tanzania as well as out of Tanzania getting together to learn ways of the Kingdom of God and how to operate them into our day to day lives so that to impact our societies spiritually, socially as well as economically. It was founded and first held on September 2017 at Mlandizi-Kibaha Coast region. Since then it has been held on September every year. Through Generation Impact youths get enough time to study the word of God (the bible), singing, dancing and having fun together as well as getting opportunity to know one another and expand network of friends as sons and daughters in the kingdom. Apart from International Apostolic Youth Summit which is held at Mlandizi there are other Generation Impact events that are conducted throughout Tanzania e.g. Generation Impact Kilimanjaro which was conducted from 25th-28th July 2019. Youths from universities, colleges and secondary schools all over Kilimanjaro and near regions got a wonderful time to study the bible, principles and culture of the kingdom of God and how to live according to them. Generation Impact exists to lead young people to real transformation as they love God and change their world. Among areas we concentrate as Generation Impact are:-

  • FINDING FREEDOM IN JESUS CHRIST
