about us

What is Generation Impact?

Generation Impact is an annual youth conference where youth from all over Tanzania, as well as, outside of Tanzania come together to learn about the Kingdom of God and grow spiritually. Young people learn to walk with Christ in their day-to-day lives in order to impact society spiritually, socially, as well as economically. This conference was founded and first held in September 2017 at Mlandizi-Kibaha Coast region. Since then, it has been held annually in September. At the conference, young people spend time studying the word of God (the bible), singing, worshipping, and having fun together. It's an opportunity to meet new people from around the country and expand their network of friends as sons and daughters in the kingdom.
Apart from this conference, known as the International Apostolic Youth Summit, there are other Generation Impact events that are conducted throughout Tanzania e.g. Generation Impact Kilimanjaro which was conducted from 25th-28th July 2019. Young people from universities, colleges and secondary schools in the Kilimanjaro area and nearby regions had a wonderful time studying the bible, it's principles, and the culture of the kingdom of God and how to live according to it.
Generation Impact exists to lead young people to real transformation as they love God and change their world. The areas we focus on are:-
